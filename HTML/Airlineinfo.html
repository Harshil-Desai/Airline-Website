Our Journey:

First Journey:

We got the delivery of our first aircraft on 28th July,2006.We made our first journey from New Delhi to Imphal via Guwahati.

Permission to Launch International Flights:

After completing five years of successful operations,we got the permission to launch international Flights.

Became Largest Airline in India in terms of market share:

On August 2012,Zenith Airlines became the largest airline in India in terms of market share surpassing ZetAirways,siz years after commencing operations

Became the first Indian airline to operate 1,500 daily flights:

In December,2019 ,We became the first airline to operate 1,500 daily flights and we expanded our fleet size to 250 aircraft

Why To Choose Zenith Airlines?

The Preferred Airline:

Zenith Airlines is not only the most efficient low fare operator domestically but is also comparable with global low cost airlines. We are constantly enhancing our engagement with our passengers to augment their travel experience. From multichannel direct sales (including online flight booking, call centers and airport counters), to online flight status checking, an exclusive Zenith app for Android, we have transformed air travel in India. Today, we are India’s most preferred airline. At Zenith, low fares come with high quality.

Great Place to Work:

Being courteous and hassle-free starts with being a hassle-free place to work. A highly engaged and motivated workforce leads to higher levels of customer service. Our state-of-the-art ‘ifly’ facility is designed to deliver a real-time training experience to all our new recruits. This training facility is considered to be one the best aviation training facilities in India. With our people-friendly culture at the heart of all we do, we continuously help the company staff find work-life balance.

Safety First:

Zenith invests heavily in safety, impeccable maintenance and a high level of expertise.Experienced pilots, engineers and maintenance crew go through rigorous training and are hand-picked for their technical knowledge and expertise.So you can rest assured that there is no cut-back in this key area of modern day flying.

Performance Guarantee:

From aircraft to crew and ground staff the focus is on performance.Each SpiceJet employee is groomed to be smart, friendly, efficient and well-informed, ensuring that any interaction will make you feel welcome and looked after.Experienced pilots, well-trained cabin crew will make every flight a comfortable one.The philosophy is no-frills but high-performance.
