Travel Advisory

Do I have to wear a face mask?

You must wear a cloth or medical mask throughout the airport, during boarding, throughout your flight, and as you leave the aircraft.

Can I choose my seat?

Yes, it’s still possible to choose your seat on selected flights. However, due to the current circumstances and for operational reasons we may need to change your seat before departure or on board.

Do I need a COVID-19 PCR test to travel?

If you are transiting via our base airport at Abu Dhabi you must present a negative COVID‑19 PCR test certificate for a test taken no more than 72 hours before departure, as well as following any requirements mandated by your final destination.

Do children need to be tested before travelling?

Children under the age of 12 do not need to present a negative test result to travel.
