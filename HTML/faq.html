Frequently Asked Questions

What is the baggage allowance?

The baggage allowance per person is 7 kg hand bag and 20 kg luggage. Additional allowance of 5kg is granted when the passenger is travelling with an infant.

How can I manage my booking?

You can head over to our home page and once you are logged in you are granted complete access to alter your booking. You can upgrade your flight ticket or even request for excess baggage allowance.

Will I be refunded after cancelling my ticket?

In normal conditions you would have to pay a fine for cancelling your ticket but in view of the current pandemic we allow you to cancel your booking without any fine.

What is a travel voucher?

A travel voucher represents the value you paid for your ticket and can be used towards future purchases with us. Your future bookings with us will have a discount based on your voucher. This voucher is only valid for 12 months from the day you bought your ticket.
